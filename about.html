About Us

About IEEE

IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. With over 400,000 members in more than 160 countries, IEEE is a leading authority in areas ranging from aerospace systems, computers, and telecommunications to biomedical engineering, electric power, and consumer electronics.

IEEE fosters technological innovation and excellence through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. IEEE provides a platform for professionals to network, collaborate, and share knowledge across all engineering disciplines.

Our Mission

The IEEE Student Branch at Illinois Institute of Technology is committed to:

  • Inspire Innovation: Encourage creative thinking and problem-solving by exposing students to emerging technologies and industry trends, supporting IIT's mission to discover, create, and solve
  • Foster Learning: Provide high-quality technical education through workshops, seminars, and hands-on projects that complement IIT's rigorous STEM-focused curriculum
  • Build Community: Create an inclusive environment where students from all backgrounds can collaborate, share ideas, and grow together, reflecting IIT's commitment to diversity and inclusion regardless of race, color, gender, sexual orientation, or national origin
  • Bridge Academia & Industry: Connect students with industry professionals and provide real-world insights into career paths and opportunities in Chicago's vibrant tech ecosystem
  • Promote Professional Development: Equip members with both technical and soft skills necessary for successful careers in technology, helping them achieve the high starting salaries Illinois Tech graduates are known for
  • Advance Technology for Good: Encourage ethical innovation and the use of technology to address societal challenges and improve lives globally
