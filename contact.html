Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

You can join IEEE as a student member through the official IEEE website. Once you're an IEEE member, reach out to us to get involved with our student branch activities!

Are events free for members?

Most of our events are free for IEEE student members. Some special events or workshops may have a nominal fee to cover materials and resources.

Can non-members attend events?

Yes! Many of our events are open to all students. However, IEEE members get priority registration and access to exclusive resources and networking opportunities.
